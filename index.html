Fire Attack Procedures: Eligibility to Effects — Checklist

A standard fire attack involves several steps, from determining eligibility to resolving the effects of fire .

1) Check Eligibility and Line of Sight (LOS)

2) Determine Fire Value of Attacking Units

3) Determine Fire Defense of Target Hex

4) Calculate Odds

5) Roll Dice and Apply Modifiers

6) Determine Losses and Effects
